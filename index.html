About Us

“Tours Bali Driver” is a local business which has been established by Ayu, who was born and raised in Gianyar. With our motto “Travel, Discover Learn”, is to provide unique experiences for each and every one of our clients. We guarantee that the quality and efficiency of our service will be superior. Our top priority is to provide the best we can offer to make your time with us an unforgettable experience.

All of our specially-selected team members are reliable and knowledgeable drivers or tour guides who truly comprehend the authentic local history and culture. When you decide to book with us, you will find a “family” who is willing to help you in any way they can. We offer a great variety of packages, including the Bali traditional tours, shore excursions, water sports, adventurous activities and many more. Please do not hesitate to contact us if you have any questions.
